About Us

Here at Toss & Roll, we believe in healthy eating. But as a college student, it can be hard to find time to make better eating choices. Toss & Roll is the newest eating option on campus, and we provide a solution. With quick wraps and salads, we've got delicious options that won't make you feel guilty.

We are a company founded in 2019 with a passion for healthy and delicious foods. As college students ourselves, we understand that food on campus needs to be quick. We have created an expedited process that gets you your food right away. Order it on the app, and get on with your day!
